Train Transportation in Nanded

Train Schedules

Nanded is well-connected by train to major cities and towns across India. Here is an overview of some important train routes and their schedules:

Nanded to Mumbai

Train Name: Nanded-Mumbai Express

Departure: 07:30 AM

Arrival: 02:00 PM

Frequency: Daily

Nanded to Delhi

Train Name: Nanded-Delhi Superfast

Departure: 05:00 PM

Arrival: 08:00 AM (Next Day)

Frequency: Every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday

Nanded to Hyderabad

Train Name: Nanded-Hyderabad Express

Departure: 11:15 AM

Arrival: 03:30 PM

Frequency: Daily

For the most current train schedules and ticket bookings, please visit the official railway website or contact the Nanded railway station.
