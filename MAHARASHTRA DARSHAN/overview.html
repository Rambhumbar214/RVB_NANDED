About Nanded

Nanded is a city in the Marathwada region of Maharashtra, India. It is known for its historical and religious significance and is a prominent center for Sikh culture.

Famous Landmarks

Hazur Sahib

The Hazur Sahib is one of the Five Takhts of Sikhism and a major religious site for Sikhs. It is known for its architectural grandeur and spiritual importance.

Nanded Fort

The Nanded Fort is a historic fortification offering panoramic views of the surrounding area. It is a popular destination for history enthusiasts and tourists.
