Bus Transportation in Nanded

Bus Routes

Nanded has a well-established bus transportation system that connects various parts of the city as well as nearby towns and villages. Below are some of the major bus routes and their details:

Route 1: Nanded to Hingoli

Stops: Nanded Bus Station, Shivaji Nagar, Hingoli Bus Station

Frequency: Every 30 minutes

Operating Hours: 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM

Route 2: Nanded to Parbhani

Stops: Nanded Bus Station, Kalamnuri, Parbhani Bus Station

Frequency: Every 45 minutes

Operating Hours: 5:30 AM - 9:30 PM

Route 3: Nanded to Latur

Stops: Nanded Bus Station, Mukhed, Latur Bus Station

Frequency: Every hour

Operating Hours: 6:30 AM - 10:30 PM

For more details on bus routes and schedules, please visit the local transport office or check the official website.
