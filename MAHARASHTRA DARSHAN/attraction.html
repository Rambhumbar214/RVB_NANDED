Historical Fort

This historical fort offers breathtaking views and a glimpse into the region’s rich history. It’s a must-visit for history enthusiasts and those who love scenic landscapes.

Botanical Gardens

Explore the lush greenery and diverse plant species at the Botanical Gardens. It’s a peaceful retreat for nature lovers and families looking to enjoy a day outdoors.

Local Museum

The Local Museum showcases artifacts and exhibits that highlight the region’s cultural heritage. It’s an educational experience for visitors of all ages.
