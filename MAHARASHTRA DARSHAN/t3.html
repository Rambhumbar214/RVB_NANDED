Sahastrakund Waterfall

About Sahastrakund Waterfall

Sahastrakund Waterfall, located in the Nanded district of Maharashtra, India, is a stunning natural attraction known for its majestic beauty and serene environment. The waterfall is situated amidst lush green surroundings, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and tourists seeking a peaceful retreat.

Features

The Sahastrakund Waterfall is renowned for its impressive height and the powerful cascade of water that flows from the top. The surrounding area is characterized by rich flora and fauna, creating a picturesque landscape that attracts photographers and adventure enthusiasts. The area around the waterfall offers various trekking and picnic opportunities, making it a great spot for outdoor activities.
